About The Organization

Our History

I am Byiringiro Kesle, the founder of EjoNiHeza, where our name reflects our mission. Byiringiro translates to "hope" in English, and it was with this hope that I embarked on a journey at the age of 18.

Driven by a desire to bring hope to those lacking opportunity, I envisioned a world where peace reigns, children find refuge in homes instead of streets, and education becomes a reality for all. EjoNiHeza, meaning "Brighter Tomorrow" in Kinyarwanda, embodies this vision.

Through our work, we strive to uplift those living in poverty, believing that a brighter tomorrow is not dependent on material wealth but on the compassion and generosity of the human heart. Anyone, regardless of their means, can make a difference in society.

Join us in our mission by volunteering your time and support. Together, let's create a brighter tomorrow for all.
