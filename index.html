Who Are We?

In the landscape of progress, and in this very first moment, there is a country named Rwanda, EjoNiHeza. We work to improve the lives of children and women in Africa. We hope for good things to happen, so that there's a bright future.

We invite you to believe in progress and to come along with us on this journey of empowerment and change.

About Us

EjoNiHeza, meaning "Brighter Tomorrow" in Kinyarwanda, embodies our mission of fostering sustainable development for enhanced livelihoods and well-being.

Founded in 2018, we have been steadfast in our commitment to championing social justice, education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.
